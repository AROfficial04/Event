Our Story

Bringing Joy to Every Celebration

EvAnia was born from a simple idea: to transform ordinary moments into extraordinary memories. Founded in 2018 by Priya and Rahul Sharma, our journey began with a passion for creating beautiful celebrations that reflect the unique personality and style of each client.

What started as a small home-based business has now grown into one of the leading event dÃ©cor and celebration services companies in the country. Our commitment to quality, attention to detail, and personalized approach has helped us create thousands of memorable experiences for our clients.
